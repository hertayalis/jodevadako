'The Voice' Winners (Both Contestants and Coaches) by Season

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Ahead, get a complete list of winners on NBC’s The Voice from Season 1 all the way to Season 15, including contestants and coaches.

Season 1

Coaches: Adam Levine, Blake Shelton, Christina Aguilera, and CeeLo Green.

Winning contestant: Javier Colon

Winning coach: Levine

In June 2011, The Voice ended Season 1 and chose the first winner of the show. Colon debuted his first studio album, Come Through for You, but it didn’t perform well on music charts. In 2016, Colon released his second album, according to Entertainment Weekly.

Season 2

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Aguilera, and Green.

Winning contestant: Jermaine Paul

Winning coach: Shelton

Paul, a former backup singer, won Season 2 of The Voice in 2012. He hasn’t released a studio album yet but continues to perform and release promotional singles.

Season 3

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Aguilera, and Green.

Winning contestant: Cassadee Pope

Winning coach: Shelton

Pope took home the top spot on Season 3 of The Voice. A year after winning the competition, she released her first studio album, Frame by Frame, in 2013, which reached No. 9 on the Billboard 200.

Season 4

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Usher, and Shakira.

Winning contestant: Danielle Bradbery

Winning coach: Blake Shelton

Bradbery won Season 4 and released her studio album, Danielle Bradbery, a few months after winning first place in the competition. Her album broke the top 20 on the Billboard 200.

Season 5

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Aguilera, and Green.

Winning contestant: Tessanne Chin

Winning coach: Levine

Season 6

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Usher, and Shakira.

Winning contestant: Josh Kaufman

Winning coach: Usher

Season 7

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Gwen Stefani, and Pharrell.

Winning contestant: Craig Wayne Boyd

Winning coach: Shelton

Season 8

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Aguilera, and Pharrell.

Winning contestant: Sawyer Fredericks

Winning coach: Pharrell

Season 9

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Stefani, and Pharrell.

Winning contestant: Jordan Smith

Winning coach: Levine

Season 10

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Aguilera, and Pharrell.

Winning contestant: Alisan Porter

Winning coach: Aguilera

Season 11

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Alicia Keys, and Miley Cyrus.

Winning contestant: Sundance Head

Winning coach: Shelton

Season 12

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Keys, and Gwen Stefani

Winning contestant: Chris Blue

Winning coach: Keys

Season 13

Coaches:  Levine, Shelton, Cyrus, and Jennifer Hudson.

Winning contestant: Chloe Kohanski

Winning coach: Shelton

Season 14

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Keys, and Kelly Clarkson.

Winning contestant: Brynn Cartelli

Winning coach: Clarkson

Cartelli won Season 14 of The Voice on Team Kelly. She made history on the show being the youngest contestant to ever win the competition at 15 years old, according to Country Living.

Season 15

Coaches: Levine, Shelton, Hudson, Clarkson, and Kelsea Ballerini (Comeback Stage).

Winning contestant: Chevel Shepherd

Winning coach: Clarkson

Season 15 of The Voice ended in Dec. 2018, according to NBC. Coaches included Levine, Clarkson, Hudson, and Shelton. For the first time, the show offered eliminated artists a chance to rejoin the competition on the Comeback Stage. Artists worked with the country singer, Kelsea Ballerini, to reclaim a spot on the show.
