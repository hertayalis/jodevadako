Early Life & More

Taking birth in a well-respected family on the 4th of June 1996, Maria Bakalova was raised in Burgas, Bulgaria. She ages gracefully and as of 2020, Maria is 24 years old. The young artist became interested in the world of entertainment when she was just a toddler.

In fact, at the age of 6, she started training as a singer and also learned how to play the flute. As per the source, she did major in acting for drama theater and a second major in flute.

Bakalova studied at the National School of Arts, graduating in the year 2015. Later, the actress moved to Sofia and took admission at the National Academy for Theatre and Film Arts. She completed her education in 2019 from there.
