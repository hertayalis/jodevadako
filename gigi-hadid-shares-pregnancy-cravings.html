Gigi Hadid Shares Her Pregnancy Cravings with Fans on Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 19, 2024
  • Gigi Hadid took to Instagram to post snapshots of her summer, also sharing insight into which dishes and snacks she's been craving during her pregnancy.
  • The photos included close-ups of cinnamon rolls, strawberries, and sushi rolls, plus landscape pics of her family's Pennsylvania farm.

Gigi Hadid has managed to keep her pregnancy relatively private, but that hasn't stopped the supermodel from sharing with fans subtle insights into her life waiting for baby.

Hadid took to Instagram yesterday to share snapshots of delectable-looking bites she's indulged in while sheltering in place at her family's farm in Pennsylvania amid the coronavirus pandemic. Gooey cinnamon rolls, fresh strawberries, and sticky sushi rolls were just a few of the cravings she shared with fans.

In between her foodie moments, Hadid posted shots of the natural landscape around the farm, where she has spent most of quarantine along with her mother, Yolanda Hadid; sister Bella Hadid; and, of course, boyfriend and the father of her baby girl, Zayn Malik.

"Summer gratitude," Hadid captioned the images, with a slew of her famous friends commenting on the hunger-inducing pics.
