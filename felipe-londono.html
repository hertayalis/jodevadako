Felipe Londono (born in 1994) is a 28 years old Colombian actor who rose to popularity after making his appearance as Nelson in the 2021 show “Wrong Side of the Tracks.” In this David Bermejo-created show, Felipe got the chance to make his romantic presence alongside Nona Sobo, and these two talented stars once again performed together in “Wrong Side of the Tracks” season 2.

After its release in 2021, “Wrong Side of the Tracks” received great hype, and people loved it. Seeing the positive response from viewers, the makers of the series decided to release the next installment, which came in 2022.

Some of you might have seen Londono in productions like “Azúcar,” “Pambelé,” “El Inquisidor,” and “¿Quién mató a Patricia Soler?”. He is also a model and occasionally walks on-ramp too.
