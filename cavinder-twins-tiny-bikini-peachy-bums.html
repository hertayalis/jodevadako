Cavinder Twins looks sensational in tiny bikini while showing off peachy bums as fans call pair 'bad

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 29, 2024

WWE hopefuls the Cavinder Twins gave fans a sneak peek into their recent vacation.

The former college basketball stars, 22, hit Paw Paw Lake in Michigan for a weekend of sun-worshipping.

After weeks of relentless wrestling preparation, Haley and Hanna showed off their impressive gym gains.

The sisters have overhauled their diet and fitness regimes since forgoing their fifth year in college to shoot for a career in the WWE.

And they showed off their toned physiques as they caught some sun with friends and family east of Lake Michigan Beach.

Sisters Natalie, Brandi, and Brooke joined the twins, with Hanna stunning in a tiny orange swimsuit.

She captioned the pics: "Coast 2[sic] coast all summer."

And fans rushed to the comments section - with some eagled-eyed followers noticing a mystery man in her final photo.

One wrote: "The last pic is so cute [crying]."

Another said: "A pair of baddies."

While a third laughed: "Damn straight [the guy in final picture] doesn't need a tag."

The Cavinders left college basketball behind after March Madness, where they helped Miami Hurricanes reach the Elite Eight.

The pair boast millions of followers on social media and want to be the next Bella Twins, the famed pro wrestling tag team who retired in 2018.

Haley and Hanna recently claimed they could take on Logan Paul when they finally make their much-anticipated WWE bow.

Asked by Logan's brother Jake whether they could beat the YouTuber-turned-boxer, Haley said: "We actually saw him at Nashville [SummerSlam] when we went.

"Like he was in the ring and I was like dang. But do I think we could? Maybe."

While a more confident Hanna added: "Oh yeah, we would absolutely kill him, kill him in the ring."
