Maggie Gyllenhaal Movies and TV Shows

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 24, 2024

Maggie Gyllenhaal, born on November 16, 1977, is an American actress and filmmaker. She comes from a family deeply involved in the film industry, with her parents being filmmakers Stephen Gyllenhaal and Naomi Achs, and her younger brother being actor Jake Gyllenhaal. Gyllenhaal started her career with minor roles in her father's films, and gained recognition for her role in the cult favorite Donnie Darko (2001), alongside her brother. She continued to showcase her talent in films like Adaptation, Confessions of a Dangerous Mind (both 2002), and Mona Lisa Smile (2003). Gyllenhaal received critical acclaim for her leading roles in Secretary (2002) and Sherrybaby (2006), earning her Golden Globe Award nominations. She gained further recognition for her role in The Dark Knight (2008) and received an Academy Award nomination for her performance in Crazy Heart (2009).

On March 11, 2024, Maggie Gyllenhaal had 4,162 Wikipedia visits, making her the #1,370 most popular actor online.
