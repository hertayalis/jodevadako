Blue Lock Chapter 245 Spoiler, Release Date, Raw Scan, and More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 19, 2024

Blue Lock fans are eagerly anticipating the next chapter of the popular Japanese manga series. The manga, which falls under the genres of sports and thriller, has been serialized in Kodansha’s Weekly Shōnen Magazine since August 2018 and has amassed 26 tankōbon volumes as of September 2023. The story follows the protagonist, Yoichi Isagi, on his intense journey in the competitive world of football.

Blue Lock Chapter 245 is scheduled for release on December 20, 2023. Fans can check the following time zones for the release timings: Pacific Time (PST) 05:30 PM, New York 08:00 PM, Indian Standard Time (IST) 6:00 AM, Philippines Standard Time (PHT) 08:30 AM, Singapore Standard Time (SST) 08:30 AM, Central European Time (CEST) 02:30 AM, Eastern European Time (EEST) 02:30 AM, Eastern Indonesian Time (EIST) 07:30 AM, Korean Standard Time (KST) 09:30 AM, Japanese Standard Time (JST) 09:30 AM, Australian Capital Territory (ACST) 10:30 AM.

As for the raw scans of Chapter 245, they are not yet available. Typically, raw scans are expected to surface 3-4 days prior to the official release date. Fans may look forward to raw scans potentially becoming available on December 17, 2023.

In the previous chapter, Chapter 244, the storyline expanded with Kaiser crafting a plan to surpass Kiyoshi at Blue Lock, promising a change in playing style compared to previous matches. The introduction of Seira against France added an intriguing element to the narrative, and Ness’s memories of encountering Kaiser hinted at their crucial role in the upcoming game.

Blue Lock enthusiasts can delve into the latest chapters in Kodansha’s Weekly Shōnen Magazine, with new episodes published every Wednesday. For English-translated volumes, readers can visit Kadonsha US. The serialized series has captivated fans with its sports drama, football intensity, and competitive spirit, leaving readers eagerly anticipating each thrilling new chapter.
