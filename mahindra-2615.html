Mahindra 2615 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Mitsubishi S3L2 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 80.4 ci [1.3 L] engine, which delivers 26 hp [19.4 kW] power at rated rpm 2500.

Mahindra 2615 Compact Utility Tractor featured with a Mitsubishi 1.3L 3-cyl diesel engine, 9 forward and 3 reverse geras, constant mesh transmission, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, hydrostatic power steering, 2,205 lbs [1000 kg] rear lift and 2315 to 2458 pounds weight.

Mahindra 2615 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a 72″ mower deck and Mahindra ML106 front-end loader.
