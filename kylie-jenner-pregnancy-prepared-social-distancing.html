Kylie Jenner's Hidden Pregnancy Primed Her for Social Distancing

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024
  • Kylie Jenner urged fans via Instagram to take the global coronavirus pandemic seriously and to practice social distancing for the next few weeks.
  • Jenner later expressed that in hindsight, keeping an extremely low profile during her pregnancy prepared her for weeks of social distancing.
  • "My pregnancy prepared me for this. I didn't leave the house for months. We got this," wrote Jenner.

If anyone's mentally prepared to be quarantined within their home for the foreseeable future, it's Kylie Jenner.

Per People, the Kylie Cosmetics CEO took to Instagram to speak with her followers on how she's handling the growing uncertainty surrounding the global pandemic. Via the posts, Jenner urged her fans to practice social distancing in an effort to combat the virus.

"Another daily reminder to take this social distancing serious and self-quarantine. I'm on day 8," wrote Jenner. "My pregnancy prepared me for this. I didn't leave the house for months. We got this."

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

Jenner famously remained mum throughout her entire pregnancy with her now two-year-old with Travis Scott, Stormi Webster. The beauty mogul didn't address the ongoing pregnancy rumors until after the birth of her daughter. She later published a sweet, intimate YouTube video—titled "To Our Daughter"— that detailed her pregnancy journey for her fans.

Beyond Jenner, Kim Kardashian West is another member of the famous family who has also stressed the fact that she is practicing social distancing in the midst of so much uncertainty surrounding coronavirus.

"[I] miss my sisters but we are all social distancing and staying away from each other all separately self quarantined. It's hard but we have to do this for our safety and for everyone else's," wrote Kardashian West. "PLEASE do not ignore the severity of the warnings to stay inside to stop the spread of this virus. We will all get through this! #TogetherApart."
