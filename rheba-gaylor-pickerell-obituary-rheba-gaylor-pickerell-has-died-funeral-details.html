Rheba Gaylor-Pickerell Obituary, Death – It is with deep sorrow that we announce the passing of Rheba Darlene Gaylor-Pickerell, a beloved soul who departed this world on February 2, 2024, at the age of 67. Born in Savannah, Georgia, Rheba touched the lives of many with her kindness, warmth, and love. Rheba is survived by her sons, Sammy Ray Gaylor, James Keith Pickerell, Troy Richard Pickerell, and Jamie Cartwright.

She leaves behind a legacy of love in her grandchildren: Steven Ray Gaylor, Brandon Queen, Mason Gaylor, Lindsey Pickerell, Trevor Pickerell, Brooklyn Pickerell, Alex Pickerell, Allee Gaylor, and Hunter Cartwright. Her great-grandchildren, Emma Gaylor and Alexis Gaylor, will forever carry a piece of her spirit.

She was predeceased by her parents, John David Mullins, Sr., and Sybil Maulden Mullins. Rheba’s memory will also be cherished by her siblings: Debra Barker, David Mullins, Dewayne Mullins, Darrell Mullins, and Donna Mullins. Her nieces and nephews join in mourning the loss of an extraordinary woman.

Friends and family are invited to pay their respects during the visitation on Sunday, February 18, 2024, from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM at Benson Funeral Home, located at 309 Hardee St, Dallas, GA 30132. A memorial service will follow at the same location at 2:00 PM. Rheba Darlene Gaylor-Pickerell will be remembered for her unwavering love, genuine kindness, and the countless lives she touched with her warm spirit. May her soul rest in eternal peace, and may her family find solace in the beautiful memories they shared.
