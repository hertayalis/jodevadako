Who Is Bob Ross Ex-Wife Jane Ross?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Robert Norman Ross known popularly as Bob Ross was an American renowned for painting, illustration and Television hosting.

He lived from October 29, 1942, to July 4, 1995.

Ross was known across America because of his television show ‘The Joy of Painting’.

Ross got married to Jane Ross in the year 1977. However, the marriage ended after a year because Ross cheated with Vivian Ridge.

Who Is Bob Ross ex-wife Jane Ross?

She was born Jane Zanardelli Ross. She was born on January 26, 1942, in Monongahela, Virginia in the United States.

Sources say she met with Ross during his days in the Airforce when he was stationed in Alaska.

The two got married in 1977 but the marriage did not last past one year.

Jane never had a child with Bob Ross, however, Bob had a son called Steve Ross who he birthed with Vivian Ridge.

Jane is said to have died on August 1, 1992at the Fair Oaks Hospital.
