American Idol Contestant Colin Stough Wiki | Bio

Colin Stough (age: 19 years; born in 2003) is a young singer from Gattman, Mississippi, who overnight became a star after participating in the famous singing show “American Idol.” The main feature of Colin is his countryside singing style and way of hitting all the notes perfectly.

As per the former HVAC Technician, his mom, Nara Johnson Goza, signed him up for the audition because she believes that her son has the potential to become the next Luke Bryan.

During the audition day, Stough made his mom proud by singing Lynyrd Skynyrd’s “Simple Man.” His performance left Katy, Lionel, and Bryan awe-inspiring.

The judges even called Nara onto the stage and thanked her that she signed Coling up for “American Idol.” In the end, the golden ticket was given to the Gattman native.

Colin Stough In Hollywood Week and Showstopper Round

The next step for the countryside guy was LA, where he had to perform in front of an audience. It was a bit overwhelming for the budding star as he had never performed on stage. Somehow Stough gathered the courage and sang Whiskey Myers’ “Stone,” making the audience fall in love with his singing.

But he did not stop there; in the next stage, the Showstopper Round, Colin showed his showman spirit by singing Chris Stapleton’s “Cold,” which earned him a place in the Top 24. Now it would be interesting to see how long his journey will continue and if he can become the first celebrity from his hometown.

Who is Colin Stough’s Girlfriend?

The “American Idol” contestant is in a relationship with a beautiful blonde lady named Emma Long. The images on Colin’s Instagram account (@colinstough) indicate that he has been dating Emma since his school days. They even went to prom together. Long is the daughter of a Policeman, and she also joined the Police force recently on March 31.

Sources have confirmed that Emma and Stough plan to get married shortly. For more updates regarding this young rockstar, follow him on Instagram and Facebook. You can also connect with him on TikTok, where he posts his viral clips.

How Old is Colin Stough?

In 2003, Colin Stough (age 19) was born in Gattman, Mississippi, United States. The first few years of Stough’s childhood were rough, as his father never cared for him and his mother, Nara Johnson Goza. Eventually, he and his mom moved in with his grandparents.

Colin was 5 when he fell in love with music and got his first guitar. Over the years, he collected many guitars and worked on his singing skills. He studied at Hatley High School, where he met the love of his life.

He never wanted to live an average life and wanted to do something extraordinary, which is why he appeared in “American Idol.” Despite his desire to achieve something special, he loves the countryside life and activities like horse riding.
