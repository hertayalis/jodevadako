See Hailey Bieber Pair a Tan Oversized Blazer with a Leather Micro Miniskirt

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 5, 2024

Hailey Bieber's latest look proves that latte brown is the fall's trendiest palette.

The model stepped out to celebrate Kim Kardashian's birthday at Funke in Beverly Hills last night, where she wore the trendy style pairing of an oversized top and tiny bottoms. Her chic party look was comprised of a white T-shirt and brown leather micro miniskirt, which she layered under a boxy warm tan blazer that ended just above her knees. She complemented the outfit with a dark red leather shoulder bag and pointy red Gucci Signoria pumps adorned with diamonds.

The Rhode founder accessorized with her signature diamond "B" necklace, as well as sculptural gold earrings and her diamond anklet. She wore her short hair tucked behind her ears, and topped off her glowing makeup with rosy blush and a glossy brown lip.

JB Lacroix//Getty Images

While Bieber is also a noted champion of the little black dress, the model chose a chocolate-brown number to celebrate the launch of Rhode in Paris last month. The clingy jersey minidress, courtesy of Saint Laurent, featured long sleeves and a wide scoop neckline. She completed the look with sheer brown tights from Calzedonia and a pair of glossy caramel slingback pumps (also from Saint Laurent), and Tiffany & Co. rose gold jewelry.

Another trend that has marked the star's fall dressing so far is all things balletcore. In late September, she embodied the style in an asymmetric baby-pink minidress by Tory Burch, which she styled with black strappy ballet flats and white ankle socks. And earlier this month, she paired a crisp white dress shirt with a tiny black miniskirt and black leather ballet flats worn with white socks.
