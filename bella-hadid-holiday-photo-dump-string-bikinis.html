See Bella Hadid Celebrate the Holidays In String Bikinis and Stylish Looks

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Bella Hadid paired her belated New Year's greeting with tons of pics from her tropical holiday vacation.

The queen of sexy and candid photo dumps started 2023 by filling her Instagram with bikini pics taken during couple's getaway to the Cayman Islands with boyfriend Marc Kalman. In the array of phots, Hadid shows off some cool looks and several bikini shots, starting with a series where she poses in a white triangular two-piece in the middle of a literal paradise.

"Happy néw year my sweets… i pray only the best for you," she captioned the carousel, where she stands in a clear pool while posing among lush greenery.

In addition to the white set, the model sported two other sizzling swimsuits. In one set of jet-skiing pics, she chose a graphic-print bikini that sported a floral smiley-face on a red and yellow background. The fun two-piece even included beads on the triangular top's strings, and she further accessorized the suit with wraparound sunglasses and gold necklaces.

The star chose a darker set from the Australian label FAE for a dip in a cold plunge pool. The suit was comprised of an underwire bralette top and high-cut bottoms printed with a brown plaid pattern. In one cute clip, Hadid giggles as she hesitates to enter the cold water.

Hadid also shared some of her casual outfit from the trip, as she embraced a fun, boho vibe to match the sunny locale. For a daytime look, she wore an eye-catching sleeveless bodysuit that ended in bermuda-length shorts, printed with multicolored daisies over a floral print background. She completed the bright ensemble with white loafers, a matching headband, and a necklace with an oversized circle pendant.

The true high-fashion moment came during a "tea party" on the beach with friends, where she paired a casual fit with avant-garde accessories. She paired a plunging red drawstring top and distressed jeans with sculpted, sole-less wooden mules from Alaïa's spring/summer 2022 collection. The vintage lover also added a baby blue and yellow Saffiano leather barrel bag from Prada, circa 2010.

Before heading to warmer weather, the Ramy actress jetted off to Aspen, where she and Kalman supported her sister Gigi at the pop-up shop for her cashmere brand Guest in Residence. She also debuted her new brownish-blonde locks during the ski trip.
