Alan Ospina Passes Away, Bassment House Music DJ

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

It has been confirmed that popular DJ Alan Ospina from Charlotte, North Carolina, passed away.

Alan was a well-known disc jockey who enjoyed a sizable fan base in the music business. On Tuesday, March 7, 2023, he suddenly passed away from an undisclosed illness.

The cherished man died while being encircled by his family, according to a family friend. His friends and admirers are devastated by the news that the cause of his death is still unknown. At the well-known Bassment House Music, where he performed as a DJ, Alan Ospina was a well-known face.

He had a deep love for music and was well known for his extraordinary talent, uniting audiences with his performances. The loss of Alan Ospina to the music business is significant, and his music will carry on his legacy as a gifted and well-liked DJ. His loved ones, friends, and admirers will always cherish and honor his memory.
