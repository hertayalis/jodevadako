Who is Maino?

New York-born Maino is a talented Maino is a hip-hop artist. He is a popular rapper and songwriter.

Active since 2006, he is very popular for his debut single “Hi Hater”. Additionally, he is also recognizable for his song, “All the Above”.

Presently, he is a prominent figure in the media for selling around 4 million digital singles globally.

His song “All the Above” got certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

Maino: Professional Life and Career

Maino initiated his career after he got imprisoned for drug-related kidnapping. He was jailed for 10 years for drug-related kidnapping.

Afterward, it was hard for him to pass time in jail. Consequently, he started writing lyrics and rapping to kill boredom.

He got out of prison in 2003. Then, he released his own record label, “Hustle Hard Entertainment”.

Later in 2005, he signed a record deal with Universal Records. But the deal didn’t go well. As he felt that the deal wasn’t good for his career, he got rid of the contract.

Afterward, in 2008, Maino signed a deal with Atlantic Records. In April 2008, he released his first single from the album, “If Tomorrow Comes”.

His debut single was named, “Hi Hater”. The single did well in the market. The same year, he released his second and third singles, “All That Above”, and “Million Bucks” respectively. “

All that Above” performed really well and his career rose to fame. The next year he released the full album.

Similarly, in 2012, he released his second studio album, “The Day After Tomorrow”. Additionally, he also released an EP in 2010 named, “Unstoppable”.
